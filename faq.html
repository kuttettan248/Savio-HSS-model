Frequently Asked Questions

What grades does Savio Higher Secondary School offer?

We offer education from Grade 5 to Grade 12.

What are the school timings?

Monday to Friday, from 8:30 AM to 9:00 PM.

How can I apply for admissions?

Admissions are open online through our website or by visiting the school office.

Does the school provide meals?

Yes, we have a clean and hygienic canteen providing healthy meals and snacks.

Are there extracurricular activities?

Yes! We offer sports, music, arts, debate, and many clubs for students to join.
